Get To Know Me

Background

My name is Allie Neal and I am an up and coming software developer. Before getting into coding, I spent a lot of time exploring different careers trying to find my calling. After completing school with a Bachelor's degree in Psychology, I decided to utilize my skills by helping our homeless animals at two different local SPCAs. I spent three years working with the animals and the public, making sure our residents found the best possible homes and were comfortable and healthy while in our care. As I became more heavily invested in data entry and managemet, discovered my passion for behind-the-scenes work. While I loved helping people and animals, there was something about being behind the computer, logging information and learning new programs that spoke to me, and I began researching tech careers. I tried practicing some coding on my own at home, and the rest is history! Software Development was the perfect way to combine my passions for helping others, creativity, and fascination with code.

Skills and Experience

I graduated from Randolph-Macon College with a degree in Psychology in 2017, and completed the Actualize Coding Bootcamp in 2021. While completing my degree, I gained experience in planning, conducting, and presenting multiple research projects. My time at the SPCA allowed me to perfect my skills in customer service and data management, including answering phones and emails, managing our social media page, and keeping shelter information up to date in our computers. Additionally I gained experience in non-profit organizations and medical care, including maintaining health logs and distributing meds. Completion of the Actualize Bootcamp solidified my skills in object-oriented programming, GitHub, CSS, HTML, various programming languages including Ruby on Rails and JavaScript, and all things Mac OS X. In addition to technological skills, I have developed strong interpersonal skills including problem solving, conflict management, communication skills, and employee training.
